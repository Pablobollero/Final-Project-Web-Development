About me

Hi, I am Pablo, Chef and a Web Developer from Argentina and Living in London since 2020.
If you are reading this and you do not know what it means “Web Developer”, this stands for create & elaborate personalized websites basically speaking.

During my time as a Chef I learn TEAMWORKING, DISCIPLINE, PROACTIVITY, TIME MANAGEMENT AND WORK BY OBJECTIVES WITH TIGHT DEAD TIMELINES.
I always dreamed between being a Chef and a Developer and I achieved my first dream for 13 long years. Now it is time to reach my second one, become a Full Stack Developer.

Eight months ago I started learning HTML5 & CSS by myself on the internet using FreeCodeCamp, codecademy, Udemy and Coursera as boot camps earning enthusiasm and basic knowledge. Right now I start a career as an Application Developer in CoderHouse, and during all the way through being an App Dev I will achieve other skills such as an excellent base of HTML5, CSS3, Git, GitHub, SASS and JavaScript ES6.

The first part of this career allows me to plan & design (through sketches and moqups), build & style (with HTML5, CSS), set up (FLEXBOX & GRIDS) to make it FULL RESPONSIVE, and using good practices applying SEO to make a totally functional online website.

For now I have enough knowledge to work as a Junior Front-End Dev with basic skills and I am glad to say I will not stop until I become a Full Stack Developer.
